Arugula Salad

Ingredients

  • 6 TBSP EVOO
  • 2 TBSP lemon juice
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Make dressing: In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large bowl, lightly dress arugula, then top with Parmesan.
